Mission

The Chrysalis Project is an autism awareness organization run by high schoolers. Our goal is to reduce the stigma around autism and other developmental disorders as well as showcase the unique beauty of them.

What we do

We release informational posts about autism to help educate the general public about it, as well as hold events to raise awareness and money for autism research groups.

We also work directly with children of autism through just spending time with them, help instructors and clinicians, and plan events for them just to have fun at.
